Тема: Normal Distribution (Нормальное распределение)

Problem 1

Условие (Problem statement)

The heights of a large group of men are normally distributed with a mean of 178 cm and a standard deviation of 4 cm. A man is selected at random from this group.

a Find the probability that he is taller than 185 cm.

A manufacturer of door frames wants to ensure that fewer than 0.005 men have to stoop to pass through the frame.

b On the basis of this group, find the minimum height of a door frame.

Рост мужчин ~ N(178, 4²). a Вероятность, что рост > 185 см. b Найти минимальную высоту дверной коробки, чтобы < 0.005 мужчин пришлось наклоняться.

Дано (Given)

  • \( X \sim \mathrm{N}(178,\ 4^{2}) \)

Найти (Find)

a \( P(X > 185) \);   b \( h \) такое, что \( P(X > h) = 0.005 \).

Решение (Solution)

Часть a. Стандартизуем:

\[ z = \frac{185 - 178}{4} = \frac{7}{4} = 1.75 \] \[ P(X > 185) = 1 - \Phi(1.75) = 1 - 0.9599 = 0.0401 \]

Часть b. Условие: \( P(X > h) < 0.005 \), то есть \( P(X \le h) \ge 0.995 \). Из таблицы критических значений: \( \Phi(z) = 0.995 \Rightarrow z = 2.5758 \).

Возвращаемся к \(X\):

\[ h = \mu + z\sigma = 178 + 2.5758 \cdot 4 = 178 + 10.30 = 188.30 \text{ см} \]

Ответ (Answer)

\[ \boxed{\text{a) } 0.0401 \qquad \text{b) } h \approx 188.3 \text{ см}} \]

Problem 2

Условие (Problem statement)

The weights of steel sheets produced by a factory are known to be normally distributed with mean 32.5 kg and standard deviation 2.2 kg.

a Find the percentage of sheets that weigh less than 30 kg.

Bob requires sheets that weigh between 31.6 kg and 34.8 kg.

b Find the percentage of sheets produced that satisfy Bob's requirements.

Вес стальных листов ~ N(32.5, 2.2²) кг. a % листов с весом < 30 кг. b % листов с весом между 31.6 и 34.8 кг.

Дано (Given)

  • \( X \sim \mathrm{N}(32.5,\ 2.2^{2}) \) кг

Найти (Find)

a \( P(X < 30) \);   b \( P(31.6 < X < 34.8) \)

Решение (Solution)

Часть a.

\[ z = \frac{30 - 32.5}{2.2} = \frac{-2.5}{2.2} \approx -1.1364 \] \[ P(X < 30) = \Phi(-1.14) = 1 - \Phi(1.14) = 1 - 0.8729 = 0.1271 \]

В процентах: ≈ 12.7%.

Часть b.

\[ z_{1} = \frac{31.6 - 32.5}{2.2} = -0.4091 \approx -0.41, \qquad z_{2} = \frac{34.8 - 32.5}{2.2} = 1.0455 \approx 1.05 \] \[ \begin{aligned} P(31.6 < X < 34.8) &= \Phi(1.05) - \Phi(-0.41) \\ &= \Phi(1.05) - (1 - \Phi(0.41)) \\ &= 0.8531 - (1 - 0.6591) \\ &= 0.8531 - 0.3409 = 0.5122 \end{aligned} \]

В процентах: ≈ 51.2%.

Ответ (Answer)

\[ \boxed{\text{a) } \approx 12.7\% \qquad \text{b) } \approx 51.2\%} \]

Problem 3

Условие (Problem statement)

The time a mobile phone battery lasts before needing to be recharged is assumed to be normally distributed with a mean of 48 hours and a standard deviation of 8 hours.

a Find the probability that a battery will last for more than 60 hours.

b Find the probability that the battery lasts less than 35 hours.

Время работы батареи ~ N(48, 8²) часов. a P(работает > 60 ч); b P(работает < 35 ч).

Дано (Given)

  • \( X \sim \mathrm{N}(48,\ 8^{2}) \) часов

Найти (Find)

a \( P(X > 60) \);   b \( P(X < 35) \)

Решение (Solution)

Часть a. \( z = \dfrac{60 - 48}{8} = 1.5 \)

\[ P(X > 60) = 1 - \Phi(1.5) = 1 - 0.9332 = 0.0668 \]

Часть b. \( z = \dfrac{35 - 48}{8} = -1.625 \)

\[ P(X < 35) = \Phi(-1.625) = 1 - \Phi(1.625) \approx 1 - 0.9479 = 0.0521 \]

Ответ (Answer)

\[ \boxed{\text{a) } 0.0668 \qquad \text{b) } \approx 0.0521} \]

Problem 4

Условие (Problem statement)

The random variable \( X \sim \mathrm{N}(24,\ \sigma^{2}) \). Given that \( P(X > 30) = 0.05 \), find

a the value of \( \sigma \),   b \( P(X < 20) \),   c the value of \(d\) so that \( P(X > d) = 0.01 \).

\( X \sim \mathrm{N}(24, \sigma^{2}) \), \(P(X > 30) = 0.05\). a Найти \(\sigma\); b \(P(X<20)\); c \(d\) такое, что \(P(X>d) = 0.01\).

Дано (Given)

  • \( X \sim \mathrm{N}(24,\ \sigma^{2}) \)
  • \( P(X > 30) = 0.05 \)

Решение (Solution)

Часть a. Из таблицы критических значений: \( P(Z > z) = 0.05 \Rightarrow z = 1.6449 \).

\[ \frac{30 - 24}{\sigma} = 1.6449 \;\Rightarrow\; \sigma = \frac{6}{1.6449} \approx 3.648 \]

Часть b. Используем \( \sigma \approx 3.648 \):

\[ z = \frac{20 - 24}{3.648} = \frac{-4}{3.648} \approx -1.0966 \] \[ P(X < 20) = 1 - \Phi(1.10) \approx 1 - 0.8643 = 0.1357 \]

Часть c. \( P(Z > z) = 0.01 \Rightarrow z = 2.3263 \).

\[ d = \mu + z\sigma = 24 + 2.3263 \cdot 3.648 \approx 24 + 8.487 \approx 32.49 \]

Ответ (Answer)

\[ \boxed{\text{a) } \sigma \approx 3.65 \quad \text{b) } \approx 0.1357 \quad \text{c) } d \approx 32.49} \]

Problem 5

Условие (Problem statement)

A machine dispenses liquid into plastic cups in such a way that the volume of liquid dispensed is normally distributed with a mean of 120 ml. The cups have a capacity of 140 ml and the probability that the machine dispenses too much liquid so that the cup overflows is 0.01.

a Find the standard deviation of the volume of liquid dispensed.

b Find the probability that the machine dispenses less than 110 ml.

Ten percent of customers complain that the machine has not dispensed enough liquid.

c Find the largest volume of liquid that will lead to a complaint.

Объём ~ N(120, σ²). \(P(\text{переполнение}) = P(X > 140) = 0.01\). a \(\sigma\); b \(P(X<110)\); c 10% жалуются — наибольший объём для жалобы.

Дано (Given)

  • \( X \sim \mathrm{N}(120,\ \sigma^{2}) \) мл
  • \( P(X > 140) = 0.01 \)

Решение (Solution)

Часть a. \( P(Z > z) = 0.01 \Rightarrow z = 2.3263 \).

\[ \frac{140 - 120}{\sigma} = 2.3263 \;\Rightarrow\; \sigma = \frac{20}{2.3263} \approx 8.60 \text{ мл} \]

Часть b. \( z = \dfrac{110 - 120}{8.60} \approx -1.163 \).

\[ P(X < 110) = 1 - \Phi(1.16) \approx 1 - 0.8770 = 0.1230 \]

Часть c. 10% жалуются на нехватку: \( P(X < v) = 0.10 \).

Из таблицы: \( \Phi(z) = 0.10 \Rightarrow z = -1.2816 \).

\[ v = 120 + (-1.2816) \cdot 8.60 \approx 120 - 11.02 \approx 108.98 \text{ мл} \]

Ответ (Answer)

\[ \boxed{\text{a) } \sigma \approx 8.60 \text{ мл} \quad \text{b) } \approx 0.123 \quad \text{c) } v \approx 109 \text{ мл}} \]

Problem 6

Условие (Problem statement)

The random variable \( X \sim \mathrm{N}(\mu,\ \sigma^{2}) \). The lower quartile of \(X\) is 20 and the upper quartile is 40. Find \( \mu \) and \( \sigma \).

\(X \sim \mathrm{N}(\mu, \sigma^{2})\), нижний квартиль = 20, верхний квартиль = 40. Найти \(\mu, \sigma\).

Решение (Solution)

По симметрии нормального распределения: \( \mu \) лежит ровно посередине между квартилями:

\[ \mu = \frac{20 + 40}{2} = 30 \]

Для верхнего квартиля: \( P(X < 40) = 0.75 \Rightarrow z = 0.6745 \).

\[ \frac{40 - 30}{\sigma} = 0.6745 \;\Rightarrow\; \sigma = \frac{10}{0.6745} \approx 14.83 \]

Ответ (Answer)

\[ \boxed{\mu = 30, \quad \sigma \approx 14.83} \]

Problem 7

Условие (Problem statement)

The heights of seedlings are normally distributed. Given that 10% of the seedlings are taller than 15 cm and 5% are shorter than 4 cm, find the mean and standard deviation of the heights.

Высота саженцев ~ N(μ, σ²). 10% выше 15 см, 5% ниже 4 см. Найти \(\mu, \sigma\).

Решение (Solution)

Составим систему из двух уравнений.

Уравнение 1: \( P(X > 15) = 0.10 \Rightarrow z_{1} = 1.2816 \):

\[ \frac{15 - \mu}{\sigma} = 1.2816 \;\Rightarrow\; 15 - \mu = 1.2816\sigma \quad (1) \]

Уравнение 2: \( P(X < 4) = 0.05 \Rightarrow z_{2} = -1.6449 \):

\[ \frac{4 - \mu}{\sigma} = -1.6449 \;\Rightarrow\; \mu - 4 = 1.6449\sigma \quad (2) \]

Сложим (1) и (2):

\[ (15 - \mu) + (\mu - 4) = (1.2816 + 1.6449)\,\sigma \] \[ 11 = 2.9265\,\sigma \;\Rightarrow\; \sigma = \frac{11}{2.9265} \approx 3.76 \]

Подставим в (2): \( \mu = 4 + 1.6449 \cdot 3.76 \approx 4 + 6.18 \approx 10.18 \).

Ответ (Answer)

\[ \boxed{\mu \approx 10.18 \text{ см}, \quad \sigma \approx 3.76 \text{ см}} \]

Problem 8

Условие (Problem statement)

A psychologist gives a student two different tests. The first test has a mean of 80 and a standard deviation of 10 and the student scored 85.

a Find the probability of scoring 85 or more on the first test.

The second test has a mean of 100 and a standard deviation of 15. The student scored 105 on the second test.

b Find the probability of a score of 105 or more on the second test.

c State, giving a reason, which of the student's two test scores was better.

Тест 1: ~ N(80, 10²), результат 85. Тест 2: ~ N(100, 15²), результат 105. a/b вероятности; c какой результат лучше.

Решение (Solution)

Часть a. \( z = \dfrac{85 - 80}{10} = 0.5 \):

\[ P(X \ge 85) = 1 - \Phi(0.5) = 1 - 0.6915 = 0.3085 \]

Часть b. \( z = \dfrac{105 - 100}{15} = \dfrac{1}{3} \approx 0.33 \):

\[ P(Y \ge 105) = 1 - \Phi(0.33) = 1 - 0.6293 = 0.3707 \]

Часть c. Чем меньше вероятность, что обычный участник набрал столько же или больше, тем лучше относительный результат. \( 0.3085 < 0.3707 \), значит результат на первом тесте был лучше (только 30.85% набрали бы 85+, тогда как 37.07% набрали бы 105+).

Ответ (Answer)

\[ \boxed{\text{a) } 0.3085 \quad \text{b) } 0.3707 \quad \text{c) первый тест лучше}} \]

Problem 9

Условие (Problem statement)

Jam is sold in jars and the mean weight of the contents is 108 grams. Only 3% of jars have contents weighing less than 100 grams. Assuming that the weight of jam in a jar is normally distributed find

a the standard deviation of the weight of jam in a jar,

b the proportion of jars where the contents weigh more than 115 grams.

Вес джема ~ N(108, σ²) г. \(P(X<100) = 0.03\). a \(\sigma\); b доля банок с весом > 115 г.

Решение (Solution)

Часть a. \( \Phi(z) = 0.03 \Rightarrow z = -1.88 \) (по таблице \( \Phi(1.88) = 0.9699 \approx 0.97 \)).

\[ \frac{100 - 108}{\sigma} = -1.88 \;\Rightarrow\; \sigma = \frac{8}{1.88} \approx 4.255 \text{ г} \]

Часть b. \( z = \dfrac{115 - 108}{4.255} \approx 1.645 \):

\[ P(X > 115) = 1 - \Phi(1.645) \approx 1 - 0.9500 = 0.0500 \]

Ответ (Answer)

\[ \boxed{\text{a) } \sigma \approx 4.26 \text{ г} \quad \text{b) } \approx 0.0500 \;(\text{или } 5\%)} \]

Problem 10

Условие (Problem statement)

The waiting time at a doctor's surgery is assumed to be normally distributed with standard deviation of 3.8 minutes. Given that the probability of waiting more than 15 minutes is 0.0446, find

a the mean waiting time,   b the probability of waiting fewer than 5 minutes.

Время ожидания ~ N(μ, 3.8²) мин, \(P(X>15) = 0.0446\). a \(\mu\); b \(P(X<5)\).

Решение (Solution)

Часть a. \( P(Z > z) = 0.0446 \Rightarrow \Phi(z) = 0.9554 \). По таблице \( \Phi(1.70) = 0.9554 \), значит \( z = 1.70 \).

\[ \frac{15 - \mu}{3.8} = 1.70 \;\Rightarrow\; \mu = 15 - 1.70 \cdot 3.8 = 15 - 6.46 = 8.54 \text{ мин} \]

Часть b. \( z = \dfrac{5 - 8.54}{3.8} = \dfrac{-3.54}{3.8} \approx -0.93 \).

\[ P(X < 5) = 1 - \Phi(0.93) = 1 - 0.8238 = 0.1762 \]

Ответ (Answer)

\[ \boxed{\text{a) } \mu \approx 8.54 \text{ мин} \quad \text{b) } \approx 0.1762} \]

Problem 11

Условие (Problem statement)

The thickness of some plastic shelving produced by a factory is normally distributed. As part of the production process the shelving is tested with two gauges. The first gauge is 7 mm thick and 98.61% of the shelving passes through this gauge. The second gauge is 5.2 mm thick and only 1.02% of the shelves pass through this gauge.

Find the mean and standard deviation of the thickness of the shelving.

Толщина полок ~ N(μ, σ²). Полка проходит через щель толщины 7 мм, если её толщина < 7 мм; через 5.2 мм — если < 5.2 мм. \(P(X<7) = 0.9861\), \(P(X<5.2) = 0.0102\). Найти \(\mu, \sigma\).

Решение (Solution)

Из таблицы \( \Phi \):

  • \( \Phi(z_{1}) = 0.9861 \Rightarrow z_{1} = 2.20 \) (т.к. \( \Phi(2.20) = 0.9861 \))
  • \( \Phi(z_{2}) = 0.0102 \Rightarrow z_{2} = -2.32 \) (т.к. \( \Phi(2.32) = 0.9898 \), \( 1 - 0.9898 = 0.0102 \))

Получаем систему:

\[ \frac{7 - \mu}{\sigma} = 2.20 \quad (1), \qquad \frac{5.2 - \mu}{\sigma} = -2.32 \quad (2) \]

Из (1) и (2): \( 7 - \mu = 2.20\sigma \), \( \mu - 5.2 = 2.32\sigma \).

Складываем:

\[ 7 - 5.2 = (2.20 + 2.32)\,\sigma \;\Rightarrow\; 1.8 = 4.52\,\sigma \;\Rightarrow\; \sigma \approx 0.398 \text{ мм} \]

Подставляем в (1):

\[ \mu = 7 - 2.20 \cdot 0.398 \approx 7 - 0.876 \approx 6.12 \text{ мм} \]

Ответ (Answer)

\[ \boxed{\mu \approx 6.12 \text{ мм}, \quad \sigma \approx 0.398 \text{ мм}} \]

Problem 12

Условие (Problem statement)

The random variable \( X \sim \mathrm{N}(14,\ 9) \). Find

a \( P(X \ge 11) \),   b \( P(9 < X < 11) \).

\(X \sim \mathrm{N}(14, 9)\) (то есть \(\sigma^{2} = 9 \Rightarrow \sigma = 3\)). a \(P(X \ge 11)\); b \(P(9 < X < 11)\).

Дано (Given)

  • \( X \sim \mathrm{N}(14,\ 3^{2}) \), \( \mu = 14, \sigma = 3 \)

Решение (Solution)

Часть a. \( z = \dfrac{11 - 14}{3} = -1 \).

\[ P(X \ge 11) = P(Z \ge -1) = \Phi(1) = 0.8413 \]

Часть b. \( z_{1} = \dfrac{9-14}{3} = -\dfrac{5}{3} \approx -1.667 \); \( z_{2} = -1 \).

\[ \begin{aligned} P(9 < X < 11) &= \Phi(-1) - \Phi(-1.667) \\ &= (1 - \Phi(1)) - (1 - \Phi(1.667)) \\ &= \Phi(1.667) - \Phi(1) \\ &\approx 0.9522 - 0.8413 = 0.1109 \end{aligned} \]

Ответ (Answer)

\[ \boxed{\text{a) } 0.8413 \quad \text{b) } \approx 0.1109} \]

Problem 13

Условие (Problem statement)

The random variable \( X \sim \mathrm{N}(20,\ 5^{2}) \). Find

a \( P(X \le 16) \),   b the value of \(d\) such that \( P(X < d) = 0.95 \).

\(X \sim \mathrm{N}(20, 5^{2})\). a \(P(X \le 16)\); b \(d\) такое, что \(P(X<d) = 0.95\).

Решение (Solution)

Часть a. \( z = \dfrac{16 - 20}{5} = -0.8 \).

\[ P(X \le 16) = \Phi(-0.8) = 1 - \Phi(0.8) = 1 - 0.7881 = 0.2119 \]

Часть b. \( \Phi(z) = 0.95 \Rightarrow z = 1.6449 \).

\[ d = 20 + 1.6449 \cdot 5 \approx 20 + 8.22 \approx 28.22 \]

Ответ (Answer)

\[ \boxed{\text{a) } 0.2119 \quad \text{b) } d \approx 28.22} \]
