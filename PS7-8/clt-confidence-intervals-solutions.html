Часть 1. Central Limit Theorem (Центральная предельная теорема)

CLT — Problem 1

Условие (Problem statement)

A sample of size 6 is taken from a normal distribution \( \mathrm{N}(10,\ 2^{2}) \). What is the probability that the sample mean exceeds 12?

Выборка \(n = 6\) из \(\mathrm{N}(10, 2^{2})\). Найти \(P(\overline{X} > 12)\).

Решение (Solution)

\( \overline{X} \sim \mathrm{N}\!\left(10,\ \dfrac{4}{6}\right) \), \( \mathrm{SE} = \dfrac{2}{\sqrt{6}} \approx 0.8165 \).

\[ z = \frac{12 - 10}{2/\sqrt{6}} = \sqrt{6} \approx 2.449 \] \[ P(\overline{X} > 12) = 1 - \Phi(2.45) \approx 1 - 0.9929 = 0.0071 \]

Ответ

\[ \boxed{P(\overline{X} > 12) \approx 0.0071} \]

CLT — Problem 2

Условие (Problem statement)

A machine fills cartons in such a way that the amount of drink in each carton is distributed normally with a mean of 40 cm³ and a standard deviation of 1.5 cm³.

a A sample of four cartons is examined. Find \(P(\overline{X} > 40.5)\).

b A sample of 49 cartons is examined. Find \(P(\overline{X} > 40.5)\).

Объём ~ N(40, 1.5²) см³. a \(n=4\), \(P(\overline{X}>40.5)\). b \(n=49\), то же.

Решение (Solution)

a) \( \overline{X} \sim \mathrm{N}(40,\ 1.5^{2}/4) \), SE = 0.75. \( z = 0.5/0.75 = 0.667 \).

\[ P(\overline{X} > 40.5) = 1 - \Phi(0.67) = 1 - 0.7486 = 0.2514 \]

b) SE = 1.5/7 ≈ 0.2143. \( z = 0.5/0.2143 \approx 2.333 \).

\[ P(\overline{X} > 40.5) = 1 - \Phi(2.33) = 1 - 0.9901 = 0.0099 \]

Ответ

\[ \boxed{\text{a) } 0.2514 \qquad \text{b) } 0.0099} \]

CLT — Problem 3

Условие (Problem statement)

The lengths of bolts produced by a machine have an unknown distribution with mean 3.03 cm and standard deviation 0.20 cm. A sample of 100 bolts is taken.

a Estimate the probability that the mean length of this sample is less than 3 cm.

b What size sample is required if the probability that the mean is less than 3 cm is to be less than 1%?

Болты: μ=3.03, σ=0.20. a n=100, \(P(\overline{X}<3)\). b Найти \(n\), чтобы \(P(\overline{X}<3) < 1\%\).

Решение (Solution)

a) По ЦПТ: \( \overline{X} \approx \mathrm{N}(3.03,\ 0.04/100) \), SE = 0.02.

\[ z = \frac{3 - 3.03}{0.02} = -1.5, \quad P(\overline{X} < 3) = 1 - \Phi(1.5) = 0.0668 \]

b) Нужно \( P(\overline{X} < 3) < 0.01 \Rightarrow z < -2.3263 \).

\[ \frac{-0.03}{0.20/\sqrt{n}} < -2.3263 \;\Rightarrow\; \frac{0.03\sqrt{n}}{0.20} > 2.3263 \] \[ \sqrt{n} > \frac{2.3263 \cdot 0.20}{0.03} = 15.509 \;\Rightarrow\; n > 240.5 \;\Rightarrow\; n \ge 241 \]

Ответ

\[ \boxed{\text{a) } 0.0668 \qquad \text{b) } n \ge 241} \]

CLT — Problem 4

Условие (Problem statement)

Forty observations are taken from a population with density \( f(x) = \dfrac{2}{9}x \) for \( 0 \le x \le 3 \), zero otherwise.

a Find the mean and variance of this population.

b Find an estimate of the probability that the mean of the 40 observations is more than 2.10.

Плотность \(f(x) = \tfrac{2}{9}x\) на \([0,3]\). 40 наблюдений. a мат.ожидание и дисперсия. b \(P(\overline{X} > 2.10)\).

Решение (Solution)

a) Считаем моменты:

\[ \mathrm{E}(X) = \int_{0}^{3} x \cdot \frac{2}{9}x\,dx = \frac{2}{9}\cdot\frac{x^{3}}{3}\bigg|_{0}^{3} = \frac{2}{9}\cdot 9 = 2 \] \[ \mathrm{E}(X^{2}) = \int_{0}^{3} x^{2}\cdot\frac{2}{9}x\,dx = \frac{2}{9}\cdot\frac{x^{4}}{4}\bigg|_{0}^{3} = \frac{2}{9}\cdot\frac{81}{4} = \frac{9}{2} = 4.5 \] \[ \mathrm{Var}(X) = 4.5 - 2^{2} = 0.5 \]

b) По ЦПТ \( \overline{X} \approx \mathrm{N}(2,\ 0.5/40) \), SE \( = \sqrt{0.0125} \approx 0.1118 \).

\[ z = \frac{2.10 - 2}{0.1118} \approx 0.894 \] \[ P(\overline{X} > 2.10) \approx 1 - \Phi(0.89) = 1 - 0.8133 = 0.1867 \]

Ответ

\[ \boxed{\text{a) } \mu = 2,\ \sigma^{2} = 0.5 \qquad \text{b) } \approx 0.1867} \]

CLT — Problem 5

Условие (Problem statement)

A fair die is rolled 35 times. a Find the approximate probability that the mean of the 35 scores is more than 4. b Find the approximate probability that the total of the 35 scores is less than 100.

Кубик 35 бросков. a \(P(\overline{X} > 4)\). b \(P(T < 100)\).

Решение (Solution)

Для кубика: \( \mu = 3.5 \), \( \sigma^{2} = \dfrac{6^{2}-1}{12} = \dfrac{35}{12} \approx 2.9167 \).

a) \( \overline{X} \approx \mathrm{N}(3.5,\ 35/(12\cdot35)) = \mathrm{N}(3.5,\ 1/12) \), SE \( \approx 0.2887 \).

\[ z = \frac{4 - 3.5}{0.2887} \approx 1.732, \quad P(\overline{X} > 4) \approx 1 - \Phi(1.73) = 1 - 0.9582 = 0.0418 \]

b) \( T \approx \mathrm{N}(35\cdot 3.5,\ 35\cdot 35/12) = \mathrm{N}(122.5,\ 102.083) \), SD ≈ 10.10.

\[ z = \frac{100 - 122.5}{10.10} \approx -2.227, \quad P(T < 100) \approx 1 - \Phi(2.23) = 0.0129 \]

Ответ

\[ \boxed{\text{a) } \approx 0.0418 \qquad \text{b) } \approx 0.0129} \]

CLT — Problem 6

Условие (Problem statement)

The 25 children in a class each roll a fair die 30 times and record the number of sixes they obtain. Find an estimate of the probability that the mean number of sixes recorded for the class is less than 4.5.

25 детей × 30 бросков, считают шестёрки. \(P(\overline{X} < 4.5)\).

Решение (Solution)

Для одного ребёнка: число шестёрок \( Y \sim \mathrm{B}(30,\ 1/6) \), \( \mathrm{E}(Y) = 5 \), \( \mathrm{Var}(Y) = 30\cdot\tfrac{1}{6}\cdot\tfrac{5}{6} = \tfrac{25}{6} \).

По ЦПТ \( \overline{Y} \approx \mathrm{N}(5,\ (25/6)/25) = \mathrm{N}(5,\ 1/6) \), SE \( \approx 0.4082 \).

\[ z = \frac{4.5 - 5}{0.4082} \approx -1.225, \quad P(\overline{Y} < 4.5) \approx 1 - \Phi(1.22) = 0.1112 \]

Ответ

\[ \boxed{\approx 0.1112} \]

CLT — Problem 7

Условие (Problem statement)

The error in mm made in measuring the length of a table has a uniform distribution over the range \([-5,\ 5]\). The table is measured 20 times. Find an estimate of the probability that the mean error is less than \(-1\) mm.

\[ z = \frac{-1 - 0}{0.6455} \approx -1.549, \quad P(\overline{X} < -1) \approx 1 - \Phi(1.55) = 0.0606 \]

Ответ

\[ \boxed{\approx 0.0606} \]

CLT — Problem 8

Условие (Problem statement)

Telephone calls arrive at an exchange at an average rate of two per minute. Over a period of 30 days a telephonist records the number of calls that arrive in the five-minute period before her break.

a Find an approximation for the probability that the total number of calls recorded is more than 350.

b Estimate the probability that the mean number of calls in the five-minute interval is less than 9.0.

Звонки 2/мин, за 5 мин в среднем 10. 30 дней. a \(P(T > 350)\). b \(P(\overline{X} < 9)\).

Каждый день \( X_{i} \sim \mathrm{Po}(10) \): \( \mu = 10 \), \( \sigma^{2} = 10 \).

\[ z = \frac{350 - 300}{17.32} \approx 2.887, \quad P(T > 350) \approx 1 - \Phi(2.89) = 0.0019 \]

b) \( \overline{X} \approx \mathrm{N}(10,\ 10/30) = \mathrm{N}(10,\ 1/3) \), SE \( \approx 0.5774 \).

\[ z = \frac{9 - 10}{0.5774} \approx -1.732, \quad P(\overline{X} < 9) \approx 1 - \Phi(1.73) = 0.0418 \]

Ответ

\[ \boxed{\text{a) } \approx 0.0019 \qquad \text{b) } \approx 0.0418} \]

CLT — Problem 9

Условие (Problem statement)

How many times must a fair die be rolled in order for there to be a less than 1% chance that the mean of all the scores differs from 3.5 by more than 0.1?

Сколько бросков нужно, чтобы \(P(|\overline{X} - 3.5| > 0.1) < 1\%\)?

Решение (Solution)

\( \mu = 3.5 \), \( \sigma^{2} = 35/12 \). По ЦПТ \( \overline{X} \approx \mathrm{N}(3.5,\ \tfrac{35}{12n}) \), SE \( = \sqrt{\tfrac{35}{12n}} \).

\[ P(|\overline{X} - 3.5| > 0.1) = 2\bigl(1 - \Phi(0.1/\mathrm{SE})\bigr) < 0.01 \] \[ \Phi(0.1/\mathrm{SE}) > 0.995 \;\Rightarrow\; \frac{0.1}{\mathrm{SE}} > 2.5758 \;\Rightarrow\; \mathrm{SE} < 0.03882 \] \[ \frac{35}{12n} < 0.001507 \;\Rightarrow\; n > \frac{35}{12\cdot 0.001507} \approx 1935.5 \]

Ответ

\[ \boxed{n \ge 1936} \]

CLT — Problem 10

Условие (Problem statement)

Heights of women: mean 175 cm, sd 2.5 cm. Heights of men: mean 177 cm, sd 2.0 cm. Samples of 40 women and 50 men are taken. Find the probability that the mean height of the men is more than 3 cm greater than the mean height of the women.

Женщины: 175/2.5, n=40. Мужчины: 177/2.0, n=50. \(P(\overline{X}_{м} - \overline{X}_{ж} > 3)\).

Решение (Solution)

Разность \( D = \overline{X}_{м} - \overline{X}_{ж} \):

\[ \mathrm{E}(D) = 177 - 175 = 2, \quad \mathrm{Var}(D) = \frac{2.0^{2}}{50} + \frac{2.5^{2}}{40} = 0.08 + 0.15625 = 0.23625 \] \[ \mathrm{SD}(D) \approx 0.486, \quad z = \frac{3 - 2}{0.486} \approx 2.058 \] \[ P(D > 3) \approx 1 - \Phi(2.06) = 1 - 0.9803 = 0.0197 \]

Ответ

\[ \boxed{\approx 0.0197} \]

CLT — Problem 11

Условие (Problem statement)

A computer rounds numbers to the nearest integer; rounding errors are independent and uniform on \([-0.5,\ 0.5]\).

a Given that 1000 numbers are added, find the probability that the total error is greater than \(+10\).

b Find how many numbers can be added together so that the probability that the magnitude of the total error is less than 10 is at least 0.95.

Ошибки ~ Uniform([-0.5,0.5]). a 1000 чисел, \(P(T > 10)\). b Сколько чисел можно сложить, чтобы \(P(|T|<10) \ge 0.95\)?

Решение (Solution)

\( \mu = 0 \), \( \sigma^{2} = 1/12 \).

a) \( T \approx \mathrm{N}(0,\ 1000/12) \), SD \( = \sqrt{1000/12} \approx 9.129 \).

\[ z = \frac{10 - 0}{9.129} \approx 1.0955, \quad P(T > 10) \approx 1 - \Phi(1.10) = 0.1357 \]

b) \( T \approx \mathrm{N}(0,\ n/12) \). Нужно \( P(|T| < 10) \ge 0.95 \):

\[ 2\Phi\!\left(\frac{10}{\sqrt{n/12}}\right) - 1 \ge 0.95 \;\Rightarrow\; \Phi\!\left(\frac{10}{\sqrt{n/12}}\right) \ge 0.975 \] \[ \frac{10}{\sqrt{n/12}} \ge 1.96 \;\Rightarrow\; \sqrt{n/12} \le \frac{10}{1.96} = 5.102 \] \[ n \le 12 \cdot 5.102^{2} \approx 312.4 \;\Rightarrow\; n \le 312 \]

Ответ

\[ \boxed{\text{a) } \approx 0.1357 \qquad \text{b) } n \le 312} \]

CLT — Problem 12

Условие (Problem statement)

An electrical company repairs very large numbers of television sets and wishes to estimate the mean time taken to repair a particular fault. The standard deviation of the repair time is 2.5 minutes. The manager wishes to ensure that the probability that the estimate differs from the true mean by less than 30 seconds is 0.95. Find how large a sample is required.

σ = 2.5 мин. Нужно \(P(|\overline{X}-\mu| < 0.5) = 0.95\). Найти \(n\).

Решение (Solution)

30 секунд = 0.5 мин. По ЦПТ \( \overline{X} \approx \mathrm{N}(\mu,\ 2.5^{2}/n) \).

\[ P(|\overline{X} - \mu| < 0.5) = 2\Phi\!\left(\frac{0.5\sqrt{n}}{2.5}\right) - 1 = 0.95 \] \[ \Phi(0.2\sqrt{n}) = 0.975 \;\Rightarrow\; 0.2\sqrt{n} = 1.96 \;\Rightarrow\; \sqrt{n} = 9.8 \;\Rightarrow\; n = 96.04 \]

Ответ

\[ \boxed{n \ge 97} \]

CI — Problem 1

Условие (Problem statement)

A random sample of size 9 is taken from a normal distribution with variance 36. The sample mean is 128. a Find a 95% confidence interval for \( \mu \). b Find a 99% confidence interval for \( \mu \).

\(n=9\), \(\sigma^{2}=36\), \(\overline{x}=128\). a 95% CI; b 99% CI.

Решение (Solution)

\( \sigma = 6 \), SE \( = 6/3 = 2 \).

a) \( z = 1.96 \): \( 128 \pm 1.96\cdot 2 = 128 \pm 3.92 = (124.08,\ 131.92) \).

b) \( z = 2.5758 \): \( 128 \pm 2.5758\cdot 2 = 128 \pm 5.15 = (122.85,\ 133.15) \).

Ответ

\[ \boxed{\text{a) } (124.08,\ 131.92) \qquad \text{b) } (122.85,\ 133.15)} \]

CI — Problem 4

Условие (Problem statement)

A normal distribution has standard deviation 15. Estimate the sample size required so that confidence intervals for \( \mu \) have width less than 2. a 90%, b 95%, c 99%.

\(\sigma=15\). Найти \(n\) для CI с шириной < 2.

Решение (Solution)

Полная ширина \( w = 2z\sigma/\sqrt{n} < 2 \Rightarrow z\sigma/\sqrt{n} < 1 \Rightarrow n > (15z)^{2} = 225z^{2} \).

a) \( z = 1.6449 \): \( n > 225 \cdot 2.7057 = 608.78 \Rightarrow n \ge 609 \).

b) \( z = 1.96 \): \( n > 225 \cdot 3.8416 = 864.36 \Rightarrow n \ge 865 \).

c) \( z = 2.5758 \): \( n > 225 \cdot 6.6347 = 1492.8 \Rightarrow n \ge 1493 \).

Ответ

\[ \boxed{\text{a) } 609 \quad \text{b) } 865 \quad \text{c) } 1493} \]

CI — Problem 9

Условие (Problem statement)

Each year the standard deviation of marks in a certain examination is 13.5 but the mean \( \mu \) fluctuates. An examiner has marks of a sample of 250 candidates with sample mean 68.4.

a What assumption must be made? b Compute the 95% CI for \( \mu \). c The actual \( \mu \) was 65.3. What conclusion?

\(\sigma=13.5\), \(n=250\), \(\overline{x}=68.4\). a Какое допущение? b 95% CI. c Реальное \(\mu=65.3\), вывод.

Решение (Solution)

a) Допущение: выборка является случайной (random sample) — то есть представительна для всей популяции экзаменующихся.

b) SE \( = 13.5/\sqrt{250} \approx 0.854 \). \( z = 1.96 \):

\[ 68.4 \pm 1.96 \cdot 0.854 = 68.4 \pm 1.674 = (66.73,\ 70.07) \]

c) Истинное \( \mu = 65.3 \) не попадает в 95% CI (66.73, 70.07). Вывод: выборка, скорее всего, не была случайной — есть систематическая ошибка (например, выбраны более сильные кандидаты).

Ответ

\[ \boxed{\text{a) случайная выборка} \quad \text{b) } (66.73,\ 70.07) \quad \text{c) выборка не случайна}} \]

CI — Problem 11

Условие (Problem statement)

Two intervals were calculated (one 95% and one 99%), unlabelled: \( (22.7,\ 27.3) \) and \( (23.2,\ 26.8) \).

Два интервала, какой 95%, найти SE и \(\overline{x}\).

c) \( \overline{x} \) — середина любого интервала: \( (22.7 + 27.3)/2 = 25 \).

Ответ

CI — Problem 16

Условие (Problem statement)

The error in measuring body length is \( \mathrm{N}(0,\ 1^{2}) \) mm.

a Find \( P(|\text{error}| < 0.4) \). b With 9 measurements, \( P(|\overline{X} - \text{true}| < 0.5) \). c Given mean of 9 readings is 22.53 mm, find 98% CI for true length.

Ошибка ~ N(0, 1²). a \(P(|err|<0.4)\). b n=9, \(P(|\overline{X} - \text{true}| < 0.5)\). c 98% CI.

Решение (Solution)

a) \( P(|Z| < 0.4) = 2\Phi(0.4) - 1 = 2\cdot 0.6554 - 1 = 0.3108 \approx 0.311 \).

b) Среднее 9 ошибок ~ \( \mathrm{N}(0,\ 1/9) \), SD \(=1/3\).

\[ P(|\overline{X}| < 0.5) = 2\Phi(0.5\cdot 3) - 1 = 2\Phi(1.5) - 1 = 2\cdot 0.9332 - 1 = 0.8664 \approx 0.866 \]

c) SE \( = 1/3 \approx 0.3333 \). \( z = 2.3263 \): \( 22.53 \pm 2.3263/3 \approx 22.53 \pm 0.775 = (21.75,\ 23.31) \) мм.

Ответ

\[ \boxed{\text{a) } 0.311 \quad \text{b) } 0.866 \quad \text{c) } (21.75,\ 23.31)} \]
